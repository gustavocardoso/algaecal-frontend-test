Can I take AlgaeCal Plus and Strontium Boost along with bone medications?

Absolutely. All bone drugs require calcium supplementation and AlgaeCal Plus is the highest quality calcium available. Strontium Boost is another important nutrient.

Will AlgaeCal Plus and Strontium Boost interfere with my prescription medications?

AlgaeCal Plus and Strontium Boost do not interfere with medications if taken 2-3 hours apart from your prescription. The exception to be aware of is the older style blood thinners such as Coumadin or Warfarin - because these drugs are both detrimental to bone health, as they block the usage of vitamin K2. Ask your doctor about switching to one of the newer anticoagulants as the newer type of anticoagulants do not block vitamin K2. You can learn more about the relationship between vitamin K2 and anti-coagulant medications in this article.

Can I buy AlgaeCal Plus in stores?

AlgaeCal products are not available in retail stores. Instead we offer our products direct-to-customer cutting out the middlemen and delivering superior quality with lower prices.

Also, our annual quota of algae is limited so entering retail stores would exceed our supply capabilities. AlgaeCal offers free shipping globally. Please email or phone us directly for orders to South Africa.

Does AlgaeCal contain pollutants or radiation because it is from the ocean?

AlgaeCal powder is USDA Certified Organic which means the harvest area and surrounds are inspected regularly for possible pollutants. Also, each lot of AlgaeCal is tested for heavy metal contamination, bacteria, molds and more. AlgaeCal Plus is made in an FDA inspected GMP plant where the highest standards are maintained.

I have trouble swallowing pills. How big are the capsules? Is it OK to open the capsules and mix it with food/beverages?

AlgaeCal Plus, AlgaeCal Basic, and Strontium Boost all use soft capsules which are ¾” long and easy to swallow. If you do have trouble swallowing them, the two-part capsules can be pulled apart. You can then sprinkle the tasteless powder into your favorite drinks and food. We recommend semi-solid foods such as smoothies, applesauce or yogurt and avoiding hot drinks as temperatures above 120 degrees Fahrenheit as it may reduce the potency of the vitamins C, D, and K2.

Please note that the AlgaeCal powder is only partially water soluble due to the many natural minerals and therefore does not dissolve completely in liquid.

Does AlgaeCal Plus cause side effect such as kidney stones or blocked arteries?

AlgaeCal does not cause side effects such as kidney stones or arterial calcification. AlgaeCal is a natural, plant-sourced calcium that is easy for your body to absorb. It’s similar to calcium you get from eating vegetables such as broccoli, kale and peas.

Studies have shown that calcium from food sources does not increase soft tissue calcification because foods that naturally contain calcium also supply other minerals, which helps transfer the calcium directly into your bones rather than soft tissues like arteries, kidneys and eyes. Additional magnesium, boron, vitamin K2 and vitamin D3 are added into AlgaeCal Plus to optimize absorption and transport to the bone rather than soft tissues. All these factors translate to efficient absorption and placement into bone tissue.

AlgaeCal is also much safer for you because it is more effective at a lower dose. At 720 mg per day of calcium, AlgaeCal Plus is increasing bone density in adults. Compare that to regular calcium supplements that have been studied at 1200 mg per day – and only slow bone loss.

Does AlgaeCal Plus cause constipation, gas, or bloating?

The vast majority of people who experience gastrointestinal side effects from regular calcium supplements have no issue with AlgaeCal. These digestive troubles usually derive from consuming rock-source calcium, which our bodies are not designed to digest. Plant calcium contains a variety of balancing minerals including magnesium, which make it more easily digested.

We recommend that you split your daily dose into breakfast and dinner time. Taking AlgaeCal with food increases absorption and reduces chances of any gastrointestinal issues.

Does Strontium Citrate cause any side effects?

Due to misinformation and confusion about the different forms of Strontium, there is a common misconception about strontium citrate (the strontium in Strontium Boost). The drug form of strontium called strontium ranelate has some side effects associated with its use, but these side effects are due to the ranelate, not the strontium. Natural strontium salts like strontium citrate have been shown in clinical trials and public usage to be completely safe. Moreover they are critically important for bone health. Confusion about the drug form versus the natural forms of strontium are so common that Lara Pizzorno, the author of Your Bones and medical writer specializing in bone health for over 24 years, wrote about it in the following blog post: Strontium Side Effects Are Overblown – Get the Facts.

Does Strontium overstate your DEXA readings?

There is a small overstatement of your DEXA bone results when taking strontium products, but about 90% of your bone density increase is a real increase in bone mass and strength. So if you were to see a 5% increase in bone density after supplementing with Strontium Boost, the actual increase amount might be only 4.5% (10% less than the actual reading) and .5% is an overstatement due to strontium’s heavier weight. In other words any increase in bone density when taking strontium is still an increase, even after reducing the increase to account for the strontium affect!

Important note: Studies show that even when you remove the effect of increasing bone density, strontium strengthens bones. Strontium increases bone strength independent of density changes!
